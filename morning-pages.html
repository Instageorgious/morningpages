Morning Pages

What is this?

Morning Pages is a daily writing practice from Julia Cameron's The Artist's Way. Write three pages of unfiltered, stream-of-consciousness words every morning. No editing. No judgment. Just words.

Writing
  • Three pages = 750 words. Write freely in the lined area.
  • Your word count never decreases — deletions don't cost you progress.
  • Auto-saves every 3 seconds to the cloud. Use save ✦ to save immediately.
  • Past days are sealed and read-only once midnight passes.
Calendar dots
  • 🤍 completed · 🦉 completed after 9 PM · 💔 missed · 🩹 repaired
  • Click any dot with writing to read that entry.
History

Tap "browse past entries" below the calendar to open month/year navigation. Click a month, then a day to jump to any entry.

Streaks & repair
  • Milestones at 3, 7, 14, 30, 90, 150, 180, 365, 500, 730, 1095 days.
  • Miss some days? Return within a window equal to your old streak length to be offered repair.
  • Write 6 pages (double days) per missed day to earn a 🩹 and restore your streak.
Night owl 🦉

Click the owl to set a custom "new day begins at" time. Writing after 9 PM earns a 🦉 on the calendar.

Bottom bar
  • ⏳ tap the hourglass to show/hide the timer.
  • ⛵ sails as you write; becomes 🏝️ at goal.
  • Page sheets stack — one per 250 words.
